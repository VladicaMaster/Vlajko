PRICES

Adult Ticket CZK 1,600.00
Student Ticket* CZK 200.00
(*Student tickets only valid up to 26 years of age)

The ticket price does not include seating. You can book a separate seat at a table together with your ticket (Categories A and B only) or you can book an entire table inclusive of tickets (Categories B, E – I and Boxes).

Guests with SEAT RESERVATION are welcome to enter the Large Hall from 8 p.m. Guests, who do not have a seat reservation can enter the Large Hal after the Ball opening ceremony at 9:30 p.m. (for capacity reasons). The Ball opening will be broadcasted live on LCD screens in the Small Hall. Thanky you for your kind understanding.

Please see below the overview of available table categories:

CATEGORY A

Gallery/side balcony of the Large Hall, on the side behind the boxes, limited view of the dance floor. Shared tables (4 to 20 seats at a table). Price per separate seat at the table: CZK 550.00 (not including ticket)

CATEGORY B1

Vestibule of the Large Hall, no direct view of the dance floor. Shared tables (up to 10 seats at a table). Price per separate seat at the table: CZK 1,200.00 (not including ticket)

CATEGORY B2

Vestibule of the Large Hall, no direct view of the dance floor.​ Tables for 10 persons. Price per table: CZK 9,500.00 (not including tickets)​

CATEGORY E1

​

Large Hall, 6th row of tables (viewed from the dance floor). A table seating 12 guests is set on an elevated floor (2nd level) and offers a good view of the dance floor.
Price per person: CZK 2,250.00 (not including ticket)

CATEGORY E

Large Hall, 4th to 6th row of tables (viewed from the dance floor). Tables seating 10 guests are set on an elevated floor (2 levels) and offer a good view of the dance floor.
Price per table: CZK 18,000.00 (not including tickets)

CATEGORY F

Large Hall, 3rd row of tables (viewed from the dance floor). Tables seat 10 guests.
Price per table: CZK 19,900.00 (not including tickets)
